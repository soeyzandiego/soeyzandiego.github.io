About Me

Hi! My name is Zoey. I was born and raised in central Illinois, about two hours south of Chicago. I'm currently based in Lower Manhattan.

I'm an undergraduate student at New York University, majoring in Game Design at Tisch School of the Arts with a minor in Child/Adolescent Mental Health Studies.

I've been making games as a hobby for about five years now. This site showcases a handful of digital games I've made over the years.

When I'm not making games, I'm playing them! And if I'm not playing games, I'm definitely building with LEGOs!
